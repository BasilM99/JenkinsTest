AdFalcon Third Party Ad Serving

Specific Information for Visitors to Sites and Applications that Use the AdFalcon Services:

  • AdFalcon may access, index, and cache requests made from publishers’ mobile websites and applications (“Publisher Sites”) to our system. When providing AdFalcon Services, AdFalcon collects data from Publisher Sites (as described in more detail below) and uses such data for a number of purposes, including providing publishers, advertisers and others with aggregate information about traffic and site usage. From the Publisher Site providers, AdFalcon may receive and use non-personally identifiable information (such as demographic data or interests) about Publisher Site visitors to provide information to advertisers and others and to help us display advertisements that may be of interest to Publisher Site visitors.
  • Third party advertisers, publishers and ad networks do not have access to Publisher Site visitors’ Personal Data, except that, if a visitor chooses to share it (for example, by entering an email address in order to receive an offer or promotional code, or providing a telephone number to receive a text message alert), then AdFalcon shares this Personal Data with the applicable advertiser only to fulfill the Publisher Site visitor’s request, and we delete this information when it is no longer needed.
