Who we are?

AdFalcon is the first mobile advertising network in the Middle East aimed to provide a specialized mobile advertising experience to advertisers, publishers, developers and mobile users across the region.

Why am I seeing this ad?

AdFalcon may collect information about your device and activities, such as apps and sites you access and visit, the ads you click or view, apps you download, etc. which may use when displaying advertising on your device.

How can I opt out from receiving interest based advertising?

AdFalcon respects your privacy, and allows you to opt-out from receiving interest-based advertising from AdFalcon on supported devices:

Mobile Device App Opt-Out

• You may opt-out by selecting the “Limit Ad Tracking” option on Apple iOS or “Opt-out of interest-based ads" on Google Android and follow the instructions provided by Apple or Google on the relevant page.

Web Sites Opt-Out

• Click on this Link to opt-out of interest based ads on your browser.
