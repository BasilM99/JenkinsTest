AdFalcon Demand Manager (ADM)


ADM is a proprietary platform developed and built by Noqoush. With over 7 years in mobile advertising in the MENA region, delivering over 9 Billion Ad requests monthly.

We have over the past two years invested in R&D. We listened and worked with our customers - both advertisers & publishers.

How does it work?


Advertisers and their brands wanted a more accurate targeting option and publishers wanted to increase their revenues. Over and above, all research showed mobile customers desried more relevant ads to interact with and increase possibility of relevant clicks or actions. With that premise, ADM was born as the future of Noqoush and its AdFalcon Advertising Platform.

By partnering with Mobile Operators and other first party data providers, ADM is in a position to unveil new revenue streams and connect to the lucrative Mobile Advertising Ecosystem.
